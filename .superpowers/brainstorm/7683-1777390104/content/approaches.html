Implementation Approaches

How we will transition from Economy to Channel Management.

A

Approach 1: Surgical Refactor

Modify existing pages (Dashboard, Admin) to swap Shop logic for Channel logic.

  • Faster to start
  • Keeps existing CSS/Theme
  • Residual "dead code"
  • Messy state management
B

Approach 2: Clean Rebuild (Recommended)

Create fresh pages (ChannelList, Config, Admin) based on Go models. Delete old economy code.

  • Type-safe models
  • No technical debt
  • Easier to maintain
  • Initial setup time

I recommend Approach 2 (Clean Rebuild) to ensure the Webapp is high-quality and directly reflects your Go backend.
